What Makes Us So Special?

Finland is a country of vast green forests, beautiful Baltic Sea islands, windswept arctic fells and thousands of blue lakes. It is also known for its remarkable features such as Finnish saunas, ski resorts and the one and only Santa Claus. Whether it is the ice melting, revealing the green, growing grass in the spring or the leaves changing color in the forests and fells when the autumn arrives, Finns find themselves feeling united with the serene beauty of nature.
